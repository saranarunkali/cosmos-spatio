Demo Scenarios

The demo video shows four emotional interaction scenarios:

Angry Interaction

A customer approaches aggressively.
The robot detects anger and increases personal space while responding calmly.

Happy Interaction

A cheerful user greets the robot.
The robot mirrors the positive energy and engages enthusiastically.

Sad Interaction

A quiet individual approaches with withdrawn posture.
The robot responds gently and offers assistance.

Frustrated Interaction

A user struggles repeatedly with a kiosk.
The robot detects frustration and proactively guides the user.
